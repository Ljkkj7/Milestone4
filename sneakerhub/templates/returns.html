Returns Policy

At SneakerHub, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied, you may return the item within 30 days of receipt for a full refund or exchange.

Return Process

  1. Contact our customer support team at support@sneakerhub.com with your order details and return reason.
  2. Our team will provide you with a return shipping label and instructions.
  3. Package the item securely and ship it back using the provided label.
  4. Once we receive the returned item, we will process your refund or exchange within 5-7 business days.

Return Conditions

  • Items must be returned in their original condition, unworn, and with all tags attached.
  • Returns must be initiated within 30 days of receipt.
  • Custom or personalized items are not eligible for return.

Refunds

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. Please allow 5-7 business days for the refund to appear in your account after we have processed it.

Exchanges

If you would like to exchange an item for a different size or color, please indicate this in your return request. We will do our best to accommodate your exchange request based on available inventory.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our returns policy or need assistance with a return, please contact our customer support team at support@sneakerhub.com.
