Shipping Information

At SneakerHub, we strive to deliver your orders quickly and efficiently. Here's what you need to know about our shipping process:

Shipping Methods

We offer standard and expedited shipping options. Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days, while expedited shipping takes 2-3 business days. Shipping times may vary based on your location and the availability of the items you ordered.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight and dimensions of your order, as well as the shipping method you choose. You can view the estimated shipping cost during the checkout process before finalizing your purchase.

International Shipping

We ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination country. Please note that international orders may be subject to customs fees and import taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient.

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status of your shipment through the carrier's website.

Shipping Issues

If you experience any issues with your shipment, such as delays or lost packages, please contact our customer support team at support@sneakerhub.com.
